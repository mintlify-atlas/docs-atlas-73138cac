## What you can do with Metabase

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Self-service analytics">
    Empower everyone in your organization to explore data and make informed decisions:

    - **Ask questions without SQL** using the visual query builder
    - **Create interactive dashboards** with click-through behavior
    - **Share insights** with your team through collections
    - **Schedule reports** to deliver data automatically
    - **Set up alerts** to stay on top of important metrics
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Embedded analytics">
    Build analytics directly into your application:

    - **Embed individual charts and dashboards** with customizable styling
    - **Full-app embedding** for a seamless analytics experience
    - **SDK for React** to embed standalone components with custom styling
    - **Multi-tenant architecture** with row-level permissions
    - **White-label** your analytics to match your brand
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Data modeling">
    Prepare your data for analysis:

    - **Create models** that clean, annotate, and combine raw tables
    - **Define metrics** that everyone can use
    - **Set up segments** for common data subsets
    - **Add descriptions** to help users understand your data
    - **Control field types** and visibility
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Open source and commercial editions

Metabase is available in multiple editions:

- **Open Source** - Free forever with core analytics features
- **Pro** - Advanced features like SSO and custom permissions
- **Enterprise** - Full-featured with audit logging, serialization, and more

<Note>
All editions can be self-hosted or run on Metabase Cloud. You can start with the Open Source edition and upgrade anytime.
</Note>
